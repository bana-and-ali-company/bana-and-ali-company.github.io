We are pleased to announce that we now provide the service of supplying bentonite to all parts of the world

Bentonite, which is an impure clay composed mainly of montmorillonite in general, consists of two main parts, calcium bentonite and sodium bentonite.
Bentonite has many uses, as it is included in the refining of used car oils and filtering raw food oils, in addition to its uses in the field of drilling wells of all kinds, as it is based on the cohesion of the walls of the well if the layers are soft or incoherent. In other words, it works on cementing the walls of the well.
